What we do — and how we do it.

Baker Benefits Administrators was founded in 2002 with a simple model: design and administer self-funded health plans, stay independent, and stay close to the work. That hasn't changed.

What has changed is the toolkit. We were at the forefront of reference-based pricing as it gained traction — and we still are. From there we built our own machinery: a warehouse of real negotiated and Medicare rates, plus our own actuarial and repricing tooling — so plan and cost decisions run on our own engine, not a vendor's black box.

We've also taken the next step with our own product, RightRoute Pay, built around the way modern healthcare actually gets bought and paid for. Brokers, employers, and HR teams come to BBA for real solutions to the real problem of financing employee benefits. We design plans hands-on and coordinate carriers and stop-loss partners on our clients' behalf.

It's the same approach we started with — just with better tools.

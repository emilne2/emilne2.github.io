[ How we keep costs down ]

Most TPAs rent their pricing. We built ours.

What your plan spends comes down to what claims get priced at. So we own that machinery — the rate data and the pricing engine behind it — instead of passing through someone else's black box. And we don't build it once and let it sit: we maintain and refresh the rates and the engine continuously, so your plan runs on current pricing. The result: lower, clearer spend, and answers you can actually get.
